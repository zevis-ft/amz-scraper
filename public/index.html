🏆 How this works

Paste ASINs, pick a marketplace, and hit Start. Each ASIN is fetched live from Amazon (with a random delay between requests to stay polite) and checked for who currently holds the Buy Box. Add your seller/brand name to get a Yes/No "you own it" column. When it's done, download a CSV of everything.
